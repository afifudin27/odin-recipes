Soto

Description

Soto is a traditional Indonesian soup mainly composed of broth, meat, and vegetables. Soto is sometimes considered Indonesia's national dish, as it is served form Sumatra to Papua, in wide range of variations. Soto is omnipresent in Indonesia, available in many warungs (small eatery or cafe) and open-air eateries on many streets corners, to fine dining restaurants and luxurious hotels.
