Sate

Description

Sate or satay is a Southeast Asian dish of seasoned, skewered, and grilled meat, served with sauce. The earliest preparations of sate is believed to have originated in Javanese cuisine, but has spread to almost anywhere in Indonesia, where it has become a national dish. Sate is often served with Peanut sauce (a sauce made from Peanut butter) and is often accompanied with lontong (a type of rice cake).
